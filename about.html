About Me

My background in Real Estate taught me the art of clear communication, creative problem-solving, and understanding what people really need — all skills I now bring to the browser window.

I’m currently sharpening my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks, learning how to create websites that are not only functional, but welcoming — like a well-designed entryway, or that perfect kitchen with just the right amount of light.

While I may be new to tech, I bring the mindset of a seasoned professional: adaptable, client-focused, and always ready to learn. I believe that good code — like a good house — should be solid, smartly designed, and built to last.

When I'm not studying code, you'll find me sketching, planning the next hike and checking out the nearest Yarn Store
